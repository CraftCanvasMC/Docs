Canvas is in the process of and is planning to do a PR party to Folia. A PR party is essentially someone opens a bunch
of PRs and submits them to a repository. One of the goals of Canvas is to at *least* have our fixes to certain Vanilla
mechanics submitted to upstream Folia, and eventually, further features and optimizations will be submitted upstream too.

The PR party is already in progress with the first 5 PRs already open, and more coming soon. The Canvas Team decided to
not open more than 5 PRs at a time, as to not overwhelm SpottedLeaf with like 30 PRs all at once. Currently, we have these
5 PRs open:

- [Fix TamableAnimal race condition](https://github.com/PaperMC/Folia/pull/426)
- [Fix Folia#421](https://github.com/PaperMC/Folia/pull/423)
  - [Click to see the related issue](https://github.com/PaperMC/Folia/issues/421)
- [Fix spectate command](https://github.com/PaperMC/Folia/pull/422)
- [Fix dialog command](https://github.com/PaperMC/Folia/pull/420)
- [Fix POI update scheduling](https://github.com/PaperMC/Folia/pull/419)

3 PRs have already been closed/merged to Folia, and more will come soon once these start being merged.
