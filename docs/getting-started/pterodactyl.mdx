## Introduction

The CanvasMC Pterodactyl egg simplifies server deployment and management on your Pterodactyl panel. This guide walks you through adding the egg to your panel, configuring it, and ensuring a smooth deployment process. The egg is designed for ease of use and compatibility with modern server requirements.

## Compatibility

The CanvasMC egg is known to be compatible with:

- Pterodactyl Panel v1.11
- Nodes using Linux-based operating systems
- Java 22

## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Log in to your panel's admin dashboard and navigate to the **Nests** section.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Get the official egg from [our gist](https://gist.github.com/Dueris/51ef8159673b363a989962c20d451a57).
    
    :::tip

    You can easily download it by clicking the "Raw" button in the top right, then pressing Ctrl+S to save the file.
    
    :::
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Click **Import Egg** button and choose the downloaded egg.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Select what nest you want to put the egg in.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    Restart your node's daemon before creating any servers with the newly imported egg.
  </Step>
</Steps>
